How it works

  1. Create a workout (free-text, sets, rep ranges).
  2. Log sets with last session’s numbers visible.
  3. Finish and view your history heatmap—or export whenever you want.

FAQ

Why another logger? Most are slow or bloated. tetsu is for people who want to lift, not manage an app.

Play Store? Coming later. Use the APK for now.

Where is my data stored? On your device. Export/Import with CSV or JSON—no lock-in.

Will you add programs/AI/timers? Only if they don’t slow down the core logging loop. Speed & simplicity first.
