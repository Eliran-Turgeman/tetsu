How it works

  1. Create a workout (free-text, sets, rep ranges).
  2. Log sets with last session’s numbers visible.
  3. Finish, review the history heatmap, and unlock achievements while chasing your personal goals.

FAQ

Why another logger? Most trade your attention or data. tetsu is for lifters who want a quiet, private notebook that stays on their device.

Play Store? Coming later. Use the APK for now.

Where is my data stored? On your device. Export/Import with CSV or JSON—no lock-in.

Will you add programs/AI/timers? Only if they don’t compromise the simple, private logging loop. Privacy & simplicity first.
